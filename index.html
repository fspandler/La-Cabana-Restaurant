La Cabana Mexican Restaurants

Serving Amazing Mexican Food Since 1975

Locally owned and operated for more than 30 years La Cabana restaurants have been committed to excellence of service and quality when preparing each and every meal for their patrons.

Founded in 1975, the first La Cabana restaurant was opened in East Bakersfield. In the following years, the restaurant has undergone various moves until settling in their current locations on White Lane and Golden State Ave.
